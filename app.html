Instructions

A phrase that reads the same way both backwards and forwards (like EvilOlive) is called a palindrome. See if you can think of a palindrome of your own by typing any word or phrase that you like in the field to the right.

You can use one of the examples provided below, or you can try your hand at making up one of your own! When you're ready, click the Find Out button to see if your olive is evil, too! (In other words, it will display your entry in reverse so that you can tell if it is a palindrome or not...)

Examples:
  • racecar
  • kayak
  • tacocat
  • Too bad...I hid a boot.
  • Go hang a salami! I'm a lasagna hog!
